# Beta Patch Notes - v0.101.0

This patch is live on the Beta Branch. Design commentary from Anthony included in original notes.

## Content

### Reworks and Reverts

#### Silent

- **Prepare -> Prepared** (revert): Was 1-cost "Discard 2 cards. Next turn, gain 2(3) Energy." Reverted to 0-cost "Draw 1(2) card(s). Discard 1(2) card(s)."

#### Necrobinder

- **Capture Spirit** (revert/buff): Reverted to previous version. "Uncommon - Skill - Cost 1 - Enemy loses 3(4) HP. Add 3(4) Souls into your Draw Pile." (was 2(3))
- **Borrowed Time** (revert/buff): Reverted to previous version. "Uncommon - Skill - Cost 0 - Apply 3 Doom to yourself. Gain 1(2) Energy." (was 6(3) Doom, 1 Energy)

#### Regent

- **Arsenal** (rework): "Power - Rare - Cost 1 - (Innate.) Whenever you create a card, gain 1 Strength." (was "Whenever you play a Colorless Card, gain 1(2) Strength.")

### Potions & Relics

- **Pendulum** (rework): "Common - Every 3 turns, draw 1 card." (was "Whenever you shuffle your Draw Pile, draw a card.")

### Enemies

- **Doormaker** (rework/nerf): Reworked. Fight is now less random. Each visual phase has its own unique power to hamper the player.

## Balance

### General

- Elites can no longer spawn on floor 6 to better match STS1
- Improved map generation consistency
- Fixed maps sometimes generating with fewer shops than intended
- Gloom Ascension modifier no longer affects ? rooms in addition to Rest Sites

### Ironclad

- **Cinder** (buff): Now Exhausts a random card in your Hand instead of the top card of the Draw Pile
- **Tremble** (buff): Now Exhausts

### Silent

- **Serpent Form** (buff): Damage increased from 4(5) -> 4(6)

### Regent

- **Falling Star** (buff): Damage increased from 7(11) -> 8(12)
- **Glitterstream** (buff): Next Turn Block increased from 4(6) -> 5(7)
- **Parry** (buff): Block gain increased from 8(11) -> 10(14)
- **Refine Blade** (buff): Forge increased from 6(10) -> 9(13)
- **Celestial Might** (buff): Upgrade changed from +2 damage -> 1 additional hit
- **Guiding Star** (buff): Now draws cards immediately instead of next turn
- **Sword Sage** (buff): No longer increases the cost of Sovereign Blade
- **Spoils of War** (changed): Now draws 2 cards, but Forge decreased from 12(17) -> 5(8)

### Defect

- **Voltaic** (nerf): Energy cost increased from 2 -> 3

### Potions & Relics

- **Tiny Mailbox** (buff): Rarity moved from Common -> Uncommon. Potions procured increased from 1 -> 2
- **Permafrost**: Rarity moved from Common -> Uncommon
- **Bag of Marbles**: Rarity moved from Uncommon -> Common
- **Red Mask**: Rarity moved from Uncommon -> Common
- **Bellows**: Rarity moved from Uncommon -> Rare
- **Lasting Candy**: Rarity moved from Rare -> Uncommon
- **Crossbow Tanx**: Now uses Free to Play instead of Costs 0 Energy
- **Vexing Puzzlebox**: Now uses Free to Play instead of Costs 0 Energy (now also only lasts until end of turn)
- **Liquid Memories**: Now uses Free to Play instead of Costs 0 Energy

### Enemies

- **Skulking Colony** (nerf): Deals less damage, damage scales slower, no longer applies Dazed status cards
- **Haunted Ship** (changed): Now applies Dazed status cards to the player
- **The Lost and Forgotten** (nerf): Stat theft no longer scales with Ascension
- **Waterfall Giant** (nerf): HP decreased from 250(260) -> 240(250)

### Events

- Disallowed Gold generating relics from showing up as the featured relic in Welcome to Wongo's
- Mad Science Skill Chaos option (Tinker Time) now uses Free to Play instead of Costs 0 Energy
