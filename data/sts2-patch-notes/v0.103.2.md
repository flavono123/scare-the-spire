# Major Update #1 - v0.103.2

## Content & Balance

### General Changes

- **Ascension 6** reworked: Gloom - "Less rest sites." -> Inflation - "Removing cards from your deck at the Merchant is more expensive."
- All shop relics now cost 25 gold less; gold-generating relics can no longer appear in the shop
- Map generation improved and made more consistent
- Deprecated cards in returning runs replaced with placeholder Status card: "Draw 1 card. Remove this from your Deck. Exhaust."

### Ironclad

- New card **Not Yet**: Rare - Skill - Cost 2 | Heal 10(13) HP. Exhaust.
- Reworks: **Dominate**, **Expect a Fight**, **Spite**, **Stoke**
- Deprecated: **Grapple**
- Numerous buffs, nerfs, and rarity changes aimed at better survivability and Exhaust synergies

### Silent

- **Blade of Ink** reworked: generates Shivs enchanted with Inky - "This card deals 2 additional damage and applies 1 Weak."
- **Acrobatics** changed from Common to Uncommon, in line with **Skim**, **Parse**, **Glimmer**, **Burning Pact**
- Goal: make Sly builds less dominant while still satisfying

### Regent

- **Arsenal** reworked: Strength based on any cards created (not just Colorless)
- **Regalite** relic: Block for any card created (not just Colorless)
- More buffs than any other character

### Necrobinder

- **Borrowed Time** reworked: "Uncommon - Cost 0 - Skill | Apply 3 Doom. Gain 1(2) Energy." -> "Uncommon - Cost 1 - Skill | Gain 4(6) energy. Cards cost an additional energy this turn."
- Pairs well with high cost cards like **Reap** and **Bury**

### Defect

- Least changes of any character, couple of buffs and nerfs

### Colorless & Shared

- Various buffs, nerfs, pool/rarity shifts to Colorless cards, relics, and potions
- **Hidden Gem** card reworked
- **Pendulum** relic reworked
- Various event option/spawn condition tweaks

### Ancients & Neow

- Balance changes to some Ancient offerings
- 5 new Neow relics:
  - **Hefty Tablet**: Choose 1 of 3 Rare cards to add to your Deck. Add 1 Injury to your Deck.
  - **Neow's Talisman**: Upgrade 1 of your Strikes and 1 of your Defends.
  - **Neow's Bones**: Upon pickup, gain 2 random Neow Relics. Add 1 random Curse to your Deck.
  - **Phial Holster**: Upon pickup, gain 1 potion slot and procure 2 random potions.
  - **Winged Boots**: You may ignore paths when choosing the next rooms to travel to 3 times.

### Enemies

- Various buffs and nerfs (notably **Skulking Colony**)
- **Doormaker** has seen the most drastic reworks

## Art

- Updated placeholder assets for many cards, relics, enemies, and **Nonupeipe** the Ancient
- New enemy animations and character move VFX
- Card affliction VFX
- Character-based energy counter VFX

## User Interface & Experience

- New **Badges** system: end-of-run markers noting achievements, easter eggs, and notable events
- **Leaderboards** revamped: friends-only scores, new scoring schema (win status > badges > speed)
- Beta and main branch leaderboards are now separate
- **Phobia Mode** setting added
- In-game feedback tool character limit increased from 500 to 8000
- Achievements and epochs no longer unlockable in Daily or Custom runs
- Various controller support improvements

## Writing

- Clarified descriptions for various cards, relics, potions, and powers
- Example: **Fairy in a Bottle** now clearer about triggering only when HP reaches 0

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed "Fatal Error" Steam cloud sync issue
- Multiple crash, softlock, and black-screen startup fixes
- Gameplay bug fixes (e.g., **Whispering Earring** relic logic)
- Controller navigation fixes
- Multiplayer: gameplay interaction fixes, state divergence fixes, Error 5008 disconnection fixes
