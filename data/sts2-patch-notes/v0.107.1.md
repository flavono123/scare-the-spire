# Major Update #2 - v0.107.1

## Highlights

- Major Update #2 brings the beta branch changes from v0.104.0 through v0.107.0 to the main branch, plus the latest v0.107.1 fixes.
- The game's random number generation was reworked after players found correlated outcomes in separate run RNG streams, including [gold:relic]Neow's Bones[/gold] curse outcomes.
- [gold]Steam Workshop[/gold] support is now live.
- The [gold]Bestiary[/gold] is now available in the [gold]Compendium[/gold].
- [gold:monster]Doormaker[/gold] has been removed from the game and replaced by [gold:monster]Aeonglass[/gold].
- [gold:monster]Infested Prism[/gold] has been reworked.

## RNG Rework

- STS2 now uses a new PRNG implementation, xoshiro256**, to avoid human-detectable correlation between separate run RNG streams.
- The issue could make unrelated outcomes partially predictive of one another. One example called out by Mega Crit was the correlation between first-act selection and the curse rolled by [gold:relic]Neow's Bones[/gold].
- This should make outcomes such as acts, rewards, events, and [gold]curse[/gold] rolls independent in practice.

## Steam Workshop

- [gold]Steam Workshop[/gold] support has officially been added.
- Mods can now be browsed, installed, and synchronized through Steam instead of being installed manually next to the executable.
- Mod creators can upload mods with Mega Crit's STS2 mod uploader.

## Bestiary And Enemy Changes

- The [gold]Bestiary[/gold] has been added to the [gold]Compendium[/gold].
  - It lists monsters the player has encountered.
  - It shows enemy moves and animations.
- [gold:monster]Doormaker[/gold] has been removed from the game.
- [gold:monster]Aeonglass[/gold] is the new Act 3 boss.
- [gold:monster]Infested Prism[/gold] has been reworked.
