[devnote:en]Drum of Battle was previously the lowest picked Uncommon Ironclad card, and generally unpopular. This rework attempts to put it into a more interesting spot and adds some synergy to Exhaust decks. Conflagration had low pick rates and middling win rates for a Rare, while also feeling more like an effect that belonged on the Silent (caring about number of card plays). It has been reworked into a simple AOE card that can act as a Strength payout. The numbers will likely need tuning to get into a desired spot, but this should feel like a more appropriate part of the Ironclad's kit.[/devnote]

## 밸런스
[devnote]현재 [gold]문을 만드는 자[/gold]를 관찰 중이며, 인게임 피드백·SNS·지표를 종합해 지켜보고 있음. 플레이어들이 적응할 시간이 필요하고, 그렇지 않으면 즉각적인 반응에 맞춰 밸런스 조정을 하게 될 위험이 있음. 수백만 런을 분석한 결과 [gold]문을 만드는 자[/gold]의 전반적인 난이도/승률은 양호한 편(킬률과 피해량 모두 Act 3의 다른 보스보다 약간 약함). 다만 특정 플레이스타일에 지나치게 불쾌한 메커니즘이 있는지는 계속 점검할 예정.[/devnote]
[devnote:en]We are currently monitoring the Doormaker and looking at a combination in-game feedback, social media posts, and metrics. We want to give players time to adjust a bit, otherwise we'll be balancing around kneejerk reactions. Currently, from looking at millions of runs, Doormaker's overall difficulty/winrate is in a good place (slightly weaker than the other Act 3 bosses both in kill rate and damage dealt). However, we do want to ensure that its mechanics aren't too abrasive against certain playstyles.[/devnote]

### 적
[devnote]전반적인 밸런스에서 현재 기본 난이도가 꽤 어려운 상태. 더 넓은 플레이어층이 접근할 수 있도록 이 기본 난이도를 지속적으로 조정하면서, 동시에 최고 승천에서는 도전적인 경험을 계속 유지하려 함. 1편과 마찬가지로 슬2의 최고 난이도 런 완주는 게임 메커니즘을 깊이 이해하는 소수 플레이어에게 기념비적인 성취로 느껴져야 함. 첫 런 클리어 시 이 승천 시스템의 의도를 전달하는 메시지를 추가. 낮은 승천은 점진적으로 조금 더 쉽게, 높은 승천은 조금 더 어렵게 만드는 과정을 시작 — 한꺼번에 바꾸지 않고 천천히.[/devnote]
[devnote:en]When it comes to general balance, the base difficulty is quite hard right now. We're continuously adjusting this baseline difficulty to make it more accessible for a wide range of players, while also continuing to deliver a challenging game at the highest Ascensions. Similarly to the first game, completing runs of Slay the Spire 2 at the highest difficulty should feel like a monumental achievement for a fraction of players devoted to understanding the ins and outs of the game's mechanics. We are slowly starting the process of making lower Ascensions a little easier, and high Ascensions slightly harder. We want to do this gradually instead of all at once.[/devnote]
