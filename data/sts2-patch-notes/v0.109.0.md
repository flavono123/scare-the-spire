# Beta Patch Notes - v0.109.0

Another beta patch is here, Slayers! This one brings a host of balance changes, bug fixes, and new Epoch art.

Traditional Chinese translation work has also begun. There is no timeline yet for official support for other languages, but Mega Crit will let players know when additional translators are needed.

Most notably, two new [gold:ancient]Neow[/gold] relics have been introduced. One grants a powerful exclusive potion, while the other adds a new quest card with beta art.

## Content & Balance

### Ancients

- Added new [gold:ancient]Neow[/gold] relic, [gold:relic]Dowsing Rod[/gold]: "Upon pickup, add 1 [gold:card]Dowsing[/gold] to your Deck."
  - [gold:card]Dowsing[/gold] — Quest, cost 0: "After entering 5 more ? rooms, transform this into [gold:card]Abundance[/gold]."
  - [gold:card]Abundance[/gold] — Ancient Skill, cost 1: "Choose 1 of 3 (Upgraded) Powers to add into your Hand. It is free to play this turn. Exhaust."
- Added new [gold:ancient]Neow[/gold] relic, [gold:relic]Neow's Sacrifice[/gold]: "Upon pickup, procure 1 [gold:potion]Ambergris[/gold] and add 1 [gold:card]Guilty[/gold] to your Deck."
  - [gold:potion]Ambergris[/gold]: "Heal for 50% of your max HP. Take an extra turn after this one."
- Reworked [gold:relic]Diamond Diadem[/gold]: "Whenever you play 2 or fewer cards in a turn, take half damage from enemies." -> "Start combat with 20 Block. Block is not removed at the start of your next turn."
- Moved [gold:relic]Sand Castle[/gold] from Pool 1 -> Pool 2, making it the second option instead of the first.
- Changed [gold:ancient]Darv[/gold]'s blessing pools for more variety; Energy relics will appear more often.
- [gold:relic]Meat Cleaver[/gold] ([red][jitter]nerf[/jitter][/red]): HP gain decreased from 9 -> 5.
- [gold:relic]Toy Box[/gold] ([green][sine]buff[/sine][/green]): relics gained increased from 4 -> 5.
- [gold:relic]Fiddle[/gold] ([green][sine]buff[/sine][/green]): card draw increased from 2 -> 3.
- Changed [gold:relic]Sere Talon[/gold] and [gold:relic]Distinguished Cape[/gold]: swapped their downsides.

### Enemies

- [gold:monster]Aeonglass[/gold] ([red][jitter]nerf[/jitter][/red]): Ebb damage decreased from 26(32) -> 22(26) to reduce its front-loaded damage.
- [gold:monster]Torch Head Amalgam[/gold] ([green][sine]buff[/sine][/green]): now starts with a new Strong Tackle move on turn 1, increasing front-loaded damage from 18(22) -> 26(32).

### Events

- [gold:relic]History Course[/gold] ([red][jitter]nerf[/jitter][/red]): now repeats only Attacks and no longer affects Skills.

### Ironclad

- [gold:card]Expect a Fight[/gold] ([green][sine]buff[/sine][/green]): no longer says "You cannot gain additional [energy:1] this turn."
- [gold:card]Primal Force[/gold] ([green][sine]buff[/sine][/green]): [gold:card]Giant Rock[/gold] token damage increased from 16(20) -> 20(24).
- Changed [gold:card]Taunt[/gold]:
  - Block gain decreased from 7(8) -> 6(7).
  - Rarity decreased from Uncommon -> Common.
- [gold:card]Bloodletting[/gold] rarity increased from Common -> Uncommon.
- [gold:card]Cruelty[/gold] rarity decreased from Rare -> Uncommon.
- [gold:card]Dominate[/gold] rarity increased from Uncommon -> Rare.

### Silent

- Reworked [gold:card]Mirage[/gold]: Uncommon Skill, cost 1(0), "Gain Block equal to [gold:power]Poison[/gold] on ALL enemies. Exhaust." -> Uncommon Skill, cost 0, "Next turn, gain 1(2) Energy."
- Reworked [gold:card]Well-Laid Plans[/gold]:
  - Uncommon Power, cost 1, "At the end of your turn, Retain up to 1(2) card(s)." -> Rare Power, cost 1(0), "At the end of your turn, you no longer discard your Hand."
  - Now also available in multiplayer; it was previously single-player only.
- Reworked [gold:card]Expertise[/gold]: "Draw cards until you have 6(7) in your hand." -> "Draw 2(3) cards. They gain Retain this turn."
- [gold:card]Outbreak[/gold] ([green][sine]buff[/sine][/green]): damage increased from 3(4) -> 4(5).
- [gold:card]Accelerant[/gold] rarity decreased from Rare -> Uncommon.

### Regent

- [gold:card]Collision Course[/gold] ([red][jitter]nerf[/jitter][/red]): damage decreased from 11(15) -> 10(14).

### Necrobinder

## Bug Fixes

### General

- The VSync setting in Graphics options now shows the correct On/Off state after restarting.
- "Power wears off" notifications no longer block mouse input.
- Placeholder Orb numbers no longer briefly appear at the start of combat.
- Scrolling menus now snap correctly at low FPS.

### Ancients

- Fixed [gold:relic]Tanx's Whistle[/gold] card preview and tooltip snapping to the top-left corner when hovered.

### Enemies

- Fixed an awkward delay when [gold]Osty[/gold] and [gold:encounter]Kaiser Crab[/gold] spawned at the start of combat.
- Fixed [gold:monster]Fabricator[/gold] animations that were not playing correctly.
- Fixed [gold:monster]Fat Gremlin[/gold] sometimes spawning without a bag of money when it should have one.

### Silent

- [gold:card]Expose[/gold] now properly triggers [gold:relic]Hand Drill[/gold].

### Necrobinder

- [gold:card]Soulbound[/gold] now shuffles [gold:card]Soul[/gold] cards into the Draw Pile instead of putting them on the bottom.

### Multiplayer

- [gold:card]One for All[/gold] no longer previews increased damage on X-cost cards.
- Fixed [gold:card]Unmovable[/gold] causing other players to receive double Block from [gold:card]Demonic Shield[/gold] when it was not the first Block card played.
- [gold:card]Imitation Learning[/gold] now clones cards before they are played instead of after, allowing enchantments such as [gold:enchantment]Swift[/gold] to apply to the cloning player.
- [gold:card]Underworld[/gold] no longer causes [gold:power]Doom[/gold] to be applied to players when enemies deal attack damage.
- Fixed [gold:card]The Ball[/gold] interacting with [gold:relic]History Course[/gold] so that [gold:relic]History Course[/gold] could incorrectly play a copy of [gold:card]The Ball[/gold] on the next turn.
- Fixed a divergence that sometimes occurred when ending combat with [gold:card]The Ball[/gold].
- [gold:card]Hibernate[/gold] no longer gives Block to dead players.
- [gold:card]Hibernate[/gold] now correctly displays stacks in its power icon.
- If [gold:card]Huddle Up[/gold] triggers a choice for another player, the playing player is no longer blocked from playing cards.
- [gold:card]The Ball[/gold] now triggers the playing player's relics, not the receiving player's.
- Fixed card effects breaking when a player dies while their own card is still resolving, such as from [gold:power]Thorns[/gold].
- Fixed a divergence that occurred if one player took a long time to enter combat.
- Player icons are now rendered above the ink circle on the map.
- Resolved an issue where deprecating Epochs prevented that player from joining multiplayer games.

## Modding

- Merged `SavedPropertySerializationCache` into `ModelIdSerializationCache`. Saved properties are now included in its hash.
- Models from non-gameplay-affecting mods are now properly excluded from the hash.
